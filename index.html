Rules & Roles

🎯 Objective

MAFIA is a social deduction game where two groups compete: the Town (civilians) and the Mafia. The town tries to eliminate all mafia members, while the mafia tries to eliminate enough townspeople to gain majority control.

🌙 Night Phase

Everyone "closes their eyes." Special roles wake up and take actions:

  • Mafia choose a target to kill
  • Doctor chooses someone to save
  • Detective investigates a player
  • Other roles use their special abilities

☀️ Day Phase

The results of the night are revealed. Players discuss who might be mafia. Any player can nominate someone, and if seconded, a vote is held. The player with the most votes is eliminated.

🔄 Cycle

Day and night alternate until one side wins. Eliminated players cannot speak but can observe the game.

🏘️ Town Wins When...

All Mafia members and Serial Killers are eliminated from the game.

🔪 Mafia Wins When...

Mafia members equal or outnumber the Town players (Mafia gains majority control).

🃏 Jester Wins When...

The Jester is voted out and eliminated by the Town during the day phase.

🔪 Serial Killer Wins When...

The Serial Killer is the last player standing, having killed all others.

💡 Town Strategy

  • Pay attention to who votes together
  • Watch for players who are suspiciously quiet
  • Trust the Detective's findings (unless they could be Godfather)
  • Don't eliminate players without solid reasoning

🎭 Mafia Strategy

  • Blend in with the Town during discussions
  • Vote with the majority to avoid suspicion
  • Claim a Town role if accused
  • Coordinate kills to eliminate key roles first

🔍 Detective Tips

  • Investigate players you're unsure about
  • Be careful revealing your role – Mafia will target you
  • Consider revealing info gradually to stay safe

🏥 Doctor Tips

  • Self-heal on the first night to stay alive
  • Try to predict who Mafia will target
  • Protect confirmed town roles like the Detective
